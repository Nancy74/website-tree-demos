Dedicated to Restoring Your Body's Natural Balance

About Me

Dr. James Mitchell

Registered Osteopath & Clinic Director

I've spent the past 18 years helping people overcome pain and get back to doing what they love. After graduating from the British School of Osteopathy (now the University College of Osteopathy) with a Master's in Osteopathy, I worked in multi-disciplinary clinics across London before opening OsteoCare.

My approach is simple: I listen carefully, examine thoroughly, and treat you as a whole person — not just a set of symptoms. I specialise in spinal conditions, sports injuries and pregnancy-related pain, but I treat patients of all ages with a wide range of musculoskeletal problems.
